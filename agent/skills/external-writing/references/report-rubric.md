# Final Report Rubric

A report passes only when all gates pass:

1. `report.md` exists, is non-empty, and matches the requested audience and language.
2. One contestable thesis appears within the first 25% and governs every major section.
3. Consequential claims have inline source URLs and do not exceed their evidence.
4. Numbers, quotes, dates, product names, and links match `fact_check.md`.
5. Counterevidence and unresolved limitations are visible.
6. Concepts arrive before use; the reader does not need to hold several unexplained taxonomies at once.
7. The prose sounds like an informed person explaining a finding, not a textbook, news rewrite, or performance of casual speech.
8. Internal workflow language, hidden reasoning, secrets, and provider payloads are absent.
9. Search/extract usage is summarized.
10. The final assistant message is concise and links to the report instead of duplicating it.

The root agent owns this check. A child agent's claim that QA passed is not evidence that the report passed.
